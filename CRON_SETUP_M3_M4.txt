CRON-JOB.ORG SETUP FOR M3 AND M4  (5 minutes, one-time)
=========================================================================
Why: GitHub's built-in scheduler can run 5–20 minutes late. Your M1 and M2
loops stay on time because cron-job.org pings them every 15 minutes.
M3 and M4 need the same. You will make EXACT COPIES of your existing M2
job — only the workflow file name in the URL changes.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — Open cron-job.org and sign in.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — Find your existing M2 job.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
It is the job whose URL ends like this:

   .../actions/workflows/4_live_m2.yml/dispatches

Open it and click "Duplicate" (creates an exact copy).

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — Turn the copy into the M3 job. Change ONLY these two fields:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Title :  M3 options trigger
  URL   :  https://api.github.com/repos/atikhalde/nse-oi-scanner/actions/workflows/5_live_m3.yml/dispatches

           (everything else stays identical to the M2 job: POST method,
            headers with your "run-trigger" PAT token, body  {"ref":"main"},
            same every-15-minutes weekday schedule)
Save.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4 — Duplicate again and turn that copy into the M4 job:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Title :  M4 options trigger
  URL   :  https://api.github.com/repos/atikhalde/nse-oi-scanner/actions/workflows/6_live_m4.yml/dispatches
Save.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 5 — You should now have 4 cron-job.org jobs total:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. ... 3_live.yml      (M1 stocks)         already existed
  2. ... 4_live_m2.yml   (M2 stocks)         already existed
  3. ... 5_live_m3.yml   (M3 options)   <-- new
  4. ... 6_live_m4.yml   (M4 options)   <-- new

Notes:
  • Same PAT token ("run-trigger") works for all 4 — do NOT create a new one.
    (Renew that PAT around day 80 of its 90-day life, as before.)
  • Nothing else needs changing: schedule times, headers, body — all copies.
  • These two jobs only START working after the 5_live_m3.yml / 6_live_m4.yml
    files are uploaded to GitHub (see _READ_ME.txt).

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
